## Editorial filters

- Prefer article URLs with a distinct slug and visible story body.
- Reject partner content, sponsored posts, awards pages, and corporate home pages.
- For ETHealthworld, reject pages marked `Brand Connect Initiative`.
- For Gulf News, reject pages marked `PARTNER CONTENT`.
- If a supplied source fails the redirect test, replace it with another validated story from the approved list above.
